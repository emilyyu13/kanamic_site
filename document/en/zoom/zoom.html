How to integrate Zoom conferencing

≪Assumptions≫

  • ・You have a Zoom account
  • ・You have a Kanamic Cloud Service account

１．After logging in to the Kanamic Cloud Service, click 「外部サービス連携」 ("Connect with External　Service") from the menu on the upper right. (See Figure 1-1).

２．In the “Zoom” field of the 「外部サービス連携」 ("Connect with External　Service") screen, click the 「連携する」 (“Connect”) button (see Figure 1-2).

Figure 1-2: "Connect with External Service” screen

３．The Zoom screen appears.
If you are not logged in to Zoom, you will be brought to the Zoom login screen. After logging in to Zoom, the following screen will appear. Examine the request and click the 「許可」 ("Allow") button (see Figure 1-3).

Figure 1-3: Zoom requests screen

４．When you return to the 「外部サービス連携」 ("Connect with External　Service") screen, the word 「連携済み」 (“Connected”) appears as seen below (Figure 1-4).

Figure 1-4: "Connect with External　Service” Screen (prior to connecting to Zoom)

*You can disconnect from Zoom by clicking the 「解除する」 (“Disconnect”) button.

Uninstalling the Kanamic Cloud Service

  • 1.Login to your Zoom Account and navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace.
  • 2.Click Manage > Installed Apps or serach for the Kanamic Cloud Service.
  • 3.Click the Kanamic Cloud Service.
  • 4.Click Uninstall.

How to initiate a zoom meeting

１．Open the digital room of the care recipient/patient for whom you wish to conduct a meeting. (See Figure 2-1-1 and Figure 2-1-2)

２．Click on 「会議予約・調整」 (“schedule/arrange meeting”) (see figure 2-2).

Figure 2-2: “Community List” Screen

３．From the 「会議予約・調整」 (“schedule/arrange meeting”) screen, next click on 「新しく会議を設定」 (“set up new meeting”).

Figure 2-3:: “schedule/arrange meeting” screen

４．Click on 「日程調整せずに設定する」 (“set up a meeting without scheduling date/time”) and under web conferencing settings select 「Zoomミーティング」 (“Zoom meeting”) (See Figure 2-4).
*You can also initiate a Zoom meeting via 「日程調整をして設定する」 (“set up a meeting and schedule date/time”).

Figure 2-4: web conferencing settings screen

５．After entering other necessary information (date and time of the event, planned participants, etc.), click the 「確認画面へ」 ("Proceed to Confirmation Screen") button at the bottom of the screen (See Figure 2-5-1). Next, double check the meeting details and click the 「通知する」 ("Notify") button to finalize the meeting (See Figure 2-5-2).
*Please enter the date and time in Japan time.

６．When the scheduled time of the meeting arrives, exactly 10 minutes ago,the 「開始」 (“Start”) button will appear on the (“schedule/arrange meeting”) screen (from (3.) above). Click this button to start the Zoom meeting. (Figure 2-6)
*Meeting time is Japan time.

Figure 2-6: The meeting should appear within the “scheduled meetings” field of the “schedule/arrange meeting” screen.
