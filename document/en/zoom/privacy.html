I Purposes for utilization of personal information

Personal information that we collect through our website is utilized for the following purposes:

  • to provide user support for products customers purchase
  • to provide services in relation to our products
  • to process inquiries and send materials
  • to provide the latest information about products customers previously purchased or about other products we offer.
  • to compile statistical information, (which does not include identifiable personal information)
  • to conduct employment screening

In cases where we seek to use personal information for a purpose which goes beyond the scope of use described above, we will notify the customer in advance and proceed only with the consent of the customer.

II Types of personal information

We collect the following personal information:

【Personal information collected in relation to inquiries】

Personal information (such as place of work, name, home address, phone number, and email address) provided by inquirers upon making inquiries to us

【Personal information related to employment screening】

Personal information provided by job applicants (such as name, gender, date of birth, age, zip code, home address, phone number, mobile phone number, email address, name of school being attended, year admitted to university, expected date of graduation, qualifications, and work history)

【Personal information collected in relation to the Kanamic Cloud Service】

Personal information (such as name, zip code, home address, and phone number) provided by applicants to our service
Personal information registered by current users of the Kanamic Cloud Service regarding the user's medical care/nursing care/social welfare services, etc.

【Personal information collected in relation to PR / IR activities】

Personal information (such as name, zip code, home address, phone number, and image data) provided by applicable individuals

【Personal information related to our officers, counsel, and employees】

CV, job history, family information, pension information, etc.

【Personal information related to shareholders】

Name, home address, shareholder number, etc.

【Sensitive personal information 】

We will not gather, store, or use sensitive personal information other than to ensure the appropriate operation of business or for other purposes that may be deemed necessary.

III Joint use (sharing) of personal information

We strive to effectively deploy business resources and deliver services with a high degree of added value, thereby enhancing enterprise value.

For this purpose, we may conduct sharing of personal information within the scope detailed below when deemed necessary. In the event that we conduct sharing of personal information, we shall exercise strict controls and supervision.
Going forward, we may make changes to how we handle the sharing of personal information. Should this occur, we will publish the changes (in advanced) through the privacy policy document on our website.

1. Purposes for joint use (sharing) of personal information

  1. (1) to provide the Kanamic Cloud Service and deliver customer support thereof
  2. (2) To improve the quality of the Kanamic Cloud Service
  3. (3) to facilitate the fulfillment of Kanamic Cloud Service contractual obligations
  4. (4) to facilitate improvements in community public health and promote the healthy upbringing of children in the community
  5. (5) to make social security less costly and to facilitate undertakings in Scientific Nursing Care.

2. Scope of joint users of personal information

The following parties, (1) - (3), are in scope for the sharing of personal information.

  1. (1) The Kanamic Cloud Service user who has acquired the personal information (this individual is referred to as “the User”)
  2. (2) A person who coordinates with “the User” to provide medical care, nursing care, or other service (to the individual who is the subject of the foregoing personal information) and who (with regards to their use of the Kanamic Cloud Network) has been designated in advanced by “the User” and has also received approval from Kanamic Network Co.,LTD. (this individual is referred to as “the Collaborating User”)
  3. (3) Kanamic Network Co.,LTD (“the Company”)
    However, “the User” and “the Collaborating User” can only use personal information pertaining to individuals to whom they themselves provide medical care, nursing care, or other service. They are unable to access or use other personal information recorded within the Kanamic Cloud Service.

3. Personal data items subject to joint use (sharing)

All personal data items entered into the Kanamic Cloud Service may be subject to sharing, but sharing is limited to the minimum extent required in order to carry out the work stipulated under “1. purposes for joint use of personal information”. In specific, any of the following may be subject to sharing: name, gender, date of birth, home address, and phone number. Additionally, data items related to the degree of care required and data items related to the provision of medical care / nursing care / social welfare services may also be subject to sharing.

4. Name of manager of jointly used (shared) personal data

Kanamic Network Co.,LTD

5. Methods of collecting personal data

Jointly used (shared) personal information is information that “the User” (or “the Collaborating User” designated by “the User”) enters into the Kanamic Cloud Service. Alternatively, jointly used (shared) personal information may also include data that “the User” contracted to have migrated into the system when “the User” began using the system.

IV Disclosure of personal information to third parties

In general, we will not provide/disclose personal information to a third party without the advance consent of the customer.
However, under the following circumstances we will disclose personal information to third parties (to the minimum extent necessary):

  1. 1. Where required by laws or regulations
  2. 2. Where necessary for the protection of the life, body, or property of a person but it is difficult to obtain prior consent
  3. 3. Where necessary in order to improve public health or to promote the healthy upbringing of children but it is difficult to obtain prior consent
  4. 4. Where it is necessary to cooperate with national or municipal governments or public organizations or parties entrusted by national or municipal governments or public organizations in performing their legal duties, and where it is feared that obtaining prior consent might obstruct the execution of the said duties.

V Anonymized information

1.Creation of anonymized information / provision of anonymized information to third parties

We create anonymized information from the Kanamic Cloud Service and provide it to third parties. In creating anonymized information, we take appropriate measures to protect the underlying personal information in order to ensure that anonymized information we generate cannot be used to identify specific individuals and to ensure that it is impossible to use the anonymized information we generate to reconstruct the personal information from which the anonymized information was originally created. We share anonymized information with third parties for the following reasons: to provide the Kanamic Cloud Service and deliver customer support thereof; to improve service quality; to facilitate improvements in community public health; to promote the healthy upbringing of children in the community; to make social security less costly; and to facilitate undertakings in Scientific Nursing Care.

2.Anonymized information items

gender, date of birth (month & year only, day is excluded; top/bottom coding used for anonymization), home address (prefecture and city only), data items related to the degree of care required, and data items related to the provision of medical care / nursing care / social welfare services

3.Method of provision of anonymized information

a password protected file is saved to an external storage media device and handed off to the recipient or the Kanamic Cloud Service’s information sharing system is used

VI Retention period for personal information

We retain personal information for 5 years (*in some cases we retain personal information for less than 5 years as stipulated by a separate contract). After the prescribed retention period expires, we will take appropriate measures to discard the data. Depending on the data medium, this may include shredding, melting, or irretrievably erasing.

VII Things to consider when sending personal information by postal mail

When personal information is delivered by postal mail (when mailing CD, USB drives, etc.), it is the responsibility of the sender to take measures such as encryption.
Moreover, should a customer mail data without encryption, we shall bare absolutely no responsibility for any information leaks that are beyond our control, such as postal accidents, etc.
Also, should a customer send data using a medium that cannot be encrypted, such as paper, we shall bare absolutely no responsibility for any information leaks that are beyond our control, such as postal accidents, etc.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies.
A cookie is a small data file that is sent from a web server to your browser and stored on your computer.
Usage of cookies on our website is in line with industry standards. Many websites use cookies to deliver useful features to users.
These cookies detect user’s computers but are not used to identify users themselves.
You can disable cookies via your browser settings but doing so may prevent you from being able to use some features of our website.

SSL

On our website, customers’ personal information is protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL).
Personal information provided by customers through our website is encrypted before being sent or received, thereby promoting a higher level of security.
If you use a browser that does not support SSL, you may not be able to access our website or input information.
*Most major browsers support SSL, so there is usually no need to bare our use of SSL in mind when using our site.
