System for “Comprehensive Support for the Disabled”, Concept

Supporting home help services related to “Comprehensive support for the disabled legislation”

Our system can be used anytime and anywhere. All that’s needed is an internet connection.

There is no cost or burden placed on customers related to server maintenance or version upgrades due to revisions of related laws.
Customers can improve business efficiency and make the most of limited time and human resources.

Usage Scenarios

【Scenario 1】
Services related to “Comprehensive support for the disabled legislation”

Inputting data on services rendered into the care recipient’s schedule is all that’s needed to generate National Health Insurance claims. This data can then be reused to issue care recipient copay invoices. There is no need to enter data two or three times.

【Scenario 2】
Interregional use

Registering community support projects that vary by municipality with “mobility support service codes” makes it possible to generate billing forms tailored to various local governments

【Scenario 3】
Facilities operated in conjunction with home-visit elderly care services

In the case of facilities operated in conjunction with home-visit elderly care services, customers can effectively deploy a limited number of helper staff and integrate scheduling with the home-visit elderly care system.

Elderly care Software System Lineup

Regional Comprehensive Care Centers

Consultation function allows users to freely draft and search consultation documents.

In-home elderly care support offices

Information sharing system well suited to improving quality in care management and broadly reducing the burden of paper-based documentation, fax, and multiple data entry.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Home-visit Services)

Mobile input becomes possible "anytime and anywhere", including during care visits or while on the move. This function takes advantage of cloud technology.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Outpatient Day Care)

Manages the schedule of visits to the care center, and makes it possible to check today’s visitors at a glance.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Residential Services, In-facility Services)

Sales support function for managing prospective residents and managing resident rooms, function for recording vitals, and other useful capabilities.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Community-Based)

Supports management of all types of services, such as group homes, small-scale multifunctional home care, night-time home-visit elderly care, and regular patrol / ad-hoc home-visit elderly / nurse care.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Welfare Equipment)

Supports both lending and sales activities with a flexible, easy-to-operate, management function.

From initial inquiry to implementation, and beyond

1Contact us

You may reach us by phone or the input form on this site.

2Follow-up from a sales representative

One of our sales representatives will contact you.

3In-person Sales Consultation

A specialized sales representative well versed in the elderly care industry with a proven track record of delivering solutions will visit your facility and explain our elderly care system and software in detail. We will identify current problems you face and areas where you can improve. And we will propose solutions using our elderly care software. Please feel free to request a consultation.

4 Contract

Please complete the application form to use our system.

5 Pre-implementation Consultations

Ahead of implementation, we provide support for migration from existing systems as well as instruction on using our elderly care software.

6In-person Usage Guidance

A member of our elderly care software implementation support team will visit your facility. The first visit will focus on explaining how to use our system and the second visit will be to explain the billing process.

7Introducing the system

Professional staff will manage all steps of the process, so you can introduce the system with confidence.

8Telephone Support

Even after the system is introduced, Kanamic Network staff continue to provide telephone support. So even those who aren’t comfortable with operating computers can be at ease.

9Training seminars

Kanamic Network conducts inhouse training sessions for users that request it once per month. We explain how to use our technology with the aid of our training materials and we field customer inquiries.
