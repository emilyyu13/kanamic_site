Community-based System, Concept

Community-based services are supported by a full lineup of elderly care support functions

These functions support a wide range of services, such as visitation services, day care for the elderly, lodging, elderly care, and nursing. Kanamic’s unique ability to provide this level of service stems from its experience providing a system specialized in the elderly care industry from as far back as elderly care insurance first came into effect.

The system supports not only billing operations for elderly care businesses, but also evaluation of the quality of the services they provide and is jampacked with important functions like care plan creation, tablet device support, schedule management, and data utilization.

Usage Scenarios

【Scenario 1】
Small scale multifunctional in-home nursing care

A wide range of services from care plan creation to elderly day care, overnight stay, home-visit elderly care, and home-visit nursing services are supported. And this support is consistent. When using the cloud in conjunction with a PC or tablet device, it’s possible to browse and input information anytime and anywhere and improve efficiency of the business. By linking with the care manager's system, discrepancies in information are prevented, and daily management of care plans vs results is made easy. We support reliable billing data creation.

【Scenario 2】
Routine care rounds/ ad-hoc nursing home elderly care/nursing

Kanamic’s system supports both integrated and collaborative business models.

The system provides total support from insurance claims to copayment billing, and supports efficiency improvements across a wide range of businesses. Management of the care rounds schedule is also supported, which helps ensure services can be provided appropriately.

Even when busy making rounds, the cloud-based system allows system users to make inputs to “Record Book II” on-the-go (using a tablet device).

Photograph data taken with mobile devices, depicting the state of ulcers, deformation of joints, and the state of incisions can also be attached to the record, allowing changes in medical condition to be recorded in detail. This information facilitates increased cooperation with doctors and other service providers, and facilitates accuracy of medical examinations.

It is also possible to access written orders and “record I” on the go to confirm the care recipient’s status.

【Scenario 3】
Small scale multifunction facilities

Staff can check care recipients’ schedules regardless of whether it’s in relation to facility visits, home-visits for elderly care, or overnight stays.

The care recipient's condition and care information can be checked at a glance, preventing developments in health conditions from being overlooked.

Having a simple system that can be used by anyone improves office efficiency and a plethora of functions provides all-round support for the various aspects of the small-scale multifunctional facility business.

【Scenario 4】
Group Homes

We provide a billing management function specialized for group homes, making carrying out complicated billing tasks easy.

In addition, even when multiple facilities are launched, centralized management at the head office is possible, so office work costs can be reduced.

Elderly care Software System Lineup

Regional Comprehensive Care Centers

Consultation function allows users to freely draft and search consultation documents.

In-home elderly care support offices

Information sharing system well suited to improving quality in care management and broadly reducing the burden of paper-based documentation, fax, and multiple data entry.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Home-visit Services)

Mobile input becomes possible "anytime and anywhere", including during care visits or while on the move. This function takes advantage of cloud technology.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Outpatient Day Care)

Manages the schedule of visits to the care center, and makes it possible to check today’s visitors at a glance.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Residential Services, In-facility Services)

Sales support function for managing prospective residents and managing resident rooms, function for recording vitals, and other useful capabilities.

Elderly Care Business Operators (Welfare Equipment)

Supports both lending and sales activities with a flexible, easy-to-operate, management function.

Comprehensive Support for People with Disabilities

Supports effective use of a limited number of helpers, such as in the case of home-visit elderly care offices.

From initial inquiry to implementation, and beyond

A specialized sales representative well versed in the elderly care industry with a proven track record of delivering solutions will visit your facility and explain our elderly care system and software in detail.
