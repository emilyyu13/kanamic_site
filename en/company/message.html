  • Top Message

Kanamic Network provides a cloud service to support our super-aged society.

In Japan, the baby boomers will turn 75 in 2025, and it is estimated that this will be accompanied by a large increase in those in need of elderly care as a result of decreased physical and cognitive functioning.


Establishing a community integrated care system at the national level is needed to support elderly who want to be able to continue living in the place they have become accustomed to and feel most at home, safe, and comfortable. As a first step, it will be important to solidify communication among the many care specialist, families, and local governments of each region.


Following from our guiding principle of using cloud technology to support regional comprehensive care within our super-aged society, our company provides ICT platforms that break through interdisciplinary barriers and enable professionals to share information in medical care, elderly care, and childcare. An ICT platform grounded in information sharing, communication, and usability allows care recipients and their families to experience a higher quality of medical and elderly care.


Going forward, we will continue to contribute to the creation of safe and prosperous communities by providing information sharing platforms that can capitalize on ICT technology to optimize collaboration in medical care, elderly care and childcare as well as business management in elderly care.
